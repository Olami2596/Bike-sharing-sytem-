Ford GoBike System Data Exploration By Tijani Abdul-hakeem¶

INTRODUCTION

This data set includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area.

What are the main features of interest in the dataset? The features of interest in the dataset include the age range, gender of the users, popular stations and least popular stations amongst users, the distance travelled by users and time spent by users.

Top ten start station¶

The visualization shows the most used stations amongst the users. This shows the locations were the bike sharing system is most popular.

Least popular stations¶

This visualizations shows the least used stations by the users. It shows the location where the company has to put more effort into getting more users.

User type¶

As seen in the visualization the number of subscribers exceeds the number of customers by a very large amount.

Gender¶

The visualization shows that there are more male users than female users.

Age and distance travelled¶

This visualization shows that the age of a user does not have any effect on the distance travelled.

Age and duration¶

The visualization shows that the age of the user has no effect on the duration spent using the bike.

Conclusions¶

The bike sharing system is popular amongst people in their late twenties and thirties. There arre more male users tha female usres. Locations that had more users and less users were also determined. It was also discovered that the age of a user had nothing to do with the time spent and distance travelled.
